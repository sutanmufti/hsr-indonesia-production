Indonesian High Speed Rail

A story map about high speed rail in Indonesia.

In 2023, Indonesia just launched its first high speed rail. It is a proud achievement and symbol of development in Indonesia. The HSR connects 2 large metropolitan cities: Jakarta, the capital; and Bandung city.

This story map will introduce you to the Indonesisan high speed rail from a map perspective. We will look into the transportation and issues related to it. We will also look into the stations and the cities that are connected by the high speed rail. We will also look into the future of the high speed rail in Indonesia.
